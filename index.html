About me

As a passionate architect with decades of experience, I believe that great design is about more than just structures—it's about creating spaces that inspire, nurture, and connect people. My journey has taken me from high school in Mangaluru to leading projects across residences, schools, commercial complexes, and more. I am driven by creativity, attention to detail, and a deep commitment to client needs. Whether collaborating with teams or guiding clients through the design process, I strive to blend functionality with beauty, always seeking to leave a positive impact on the communities I serve. Outside of architecture, I find inspiration in nature, books, and new adventures, which continually fuel my creativity and vision.
